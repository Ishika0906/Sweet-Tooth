Biscoff Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 250g Biscoff biscuits
  • 100g melted butter
  • 500g cream cheese
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 300ml heavy cream
  • 150g Biscoff spread
  • Extra Biscoff biscuits for topping

Instructions

  1. Crush Biscoff biscuits and mix with melted butter. Press into a cake tin and refrigerate.
  2. Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Whip heavy cream separately, then fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Spread the mixture over the biscuit base and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  5. Warm the Biscoff spread slightly and drizzle over the cheesecake.
  6. Decorate with extra crushed biscuits and serve chilled.
