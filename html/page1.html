Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter (melted)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk (for glaze)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

What are Cinnamon Rolls?

Cinnamon rolls are sweet, soft pastries made from yeasted dough, rolled with a cinnamon-sugar filling and topped with a creamy glaze. These delightful treats are perfect for breakfast or dessert and have a warm, comforting flavor.

Instructions

  1. Mix warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, egg, and melted butter. Add yeast mixture and knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out dough into a rectangle, spread softened butter, and sprinkle brown sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Roll up the dough and cut into slices.
  6. Place in a baking dish and let rise for another 30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Mix powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk to make the glaze.
  9. Drizzle glaze over warm cinnamon rolls and enjoy!
